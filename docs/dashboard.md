## Overview

The dashboard provides a real-time snapshot of your HR operations. What you see depends on your role:

| Role | Dashboard Content |
|------|-------------------|
| Staff | Personal stats, own pending requests |
| Manager | Team stats, team pending approvals |
| HR/Admin | Company-wide stats, all pending items |

---

## Statistics Cards

### Total Employees
Displays the count of active employees in the organization (or your team for managers).

### Pending Leave Requests
Shows leave requests awaiting approval. Click to navigate to leave management.

### Pending Expenses
Shows expense claims awaiting approval. Click to navigate to expense claims.

### Monthly Payroll
Displays the total payroll amount for the current month (Admin/HR only).

---

## Recent Activity

The activity feed shows recent actions:

- New employee registrations
- Leave request submissions
- Leave approvals/rejections
- Expense submissions
- Payslip generations

---

## Quick Actions

Depending on your role, you'll see quick action buttons:

### For Staff
- **Apply for Leave** - Submit a new leave request
- **Submit Expense** - File an expense claim
- **View Payslips** - Access your payslips

### For Managers
- **Approve Requests** - Review pending team requests
- **Team Overview** - View team members

### For HR/Admin
- **Add Employee** - Create new employee record
- **Run Payroll** - Process monthly payroll
- **Generate Reports** - Export HR data

---

## Filtering

Use the date range picker to filter dashboard data by period:

- This Week
- This Month
- This Quarter
- Custom Range
