## Singapore CPF Rates

### Contribution Rates by Age

| Age Group | Employee | Employer | Total |
|-----------|----------|----------|-------|
| 55 and below | 20% | 17% | 37% |
| Above 55 to 60 | 18% | 16% | 34% |
| Above 60 to 65 | 12.5% | 12.5% | 25% |
| Above 65 to 70 | 7.5% | 10% | 17.5% |
| Above 70 | 5% | 7.5% | 12.5% |

### CPF Ceilings

| Type | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Ordinary Wage (OW) | $8,000 |
| Annual Ordinary Wage | $102,000 |
| Additional Wage (AW) | Varies |

## Viewing Payslips

### As an Employee

1. Navigate to **Payroll** > **My Payslips**
2. Select the month/year
3. View payslip details
4. Click **Download PDF**

### Payslip Details

- Employee information
- Pay period
- Earnings breakdown
- Deductions breakdown
- CPF contributions (employee + employer)
- Net salary

---

## Running Payroll (HR/Admin)

### Monthly Process

1. Go to **Payroll** > **Run Payroll**
2. Select pay period (month/year)
3. Review employee list
4. System calculates:
   - CPF contributions
   - Deductions
   - Net pay
5. Click **Generate Payslips**
6. Review and confirm
7. Mark as **Paid** when processed

### Payroll Statuses

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Draft | Payslip generated, not finalized |
| Processed | Finalized, pending payment |
| Paid | Payment completed |

## Salary Configuration

### Setting Up Salary (HR/Admin)

1. Navigate to employee profile
2. Click **Salary Info**
3. Enter:
   - Basic salary
   - Allowances
   - CPF applicable (Yes/No)
4. Save changes

### Salary History
All salary changes are logged with:
- Effective date
- Previous amount
- New amount
- Changed by
