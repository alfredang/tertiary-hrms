## Description

Tertiary HR Portal is a comprehensive HR management system designed for small and medium businesses in Singapore. Manage your workforce efficiently from anywhere — leave requests, expense claims, payroll, and more, all in one app.

**Key Features:**

• Leave Management — Apply for annual leave, medical leave, or half-day leave (AM/PM). Managers can approve, reject, or reset requests in real time.

• Expense Claims — Submit expense claims with receipt uploads, track approval status, and manage reimbursements.

• Payroll & Payslips — View monthly payslips with full CPF breakdown. Admins can generate payroll and upload salary data via Excel.

• Staff Directory — Browse employee profiles, departments, and contact details at a glance.

• Personal Calendar — Plan your schedule with color-coded events. Leave days are automatically reflected.

• Dashboard — Get a real-time overview of your leave balances, pending requests, and quick actions.

• Role-Based Access — Staff, Manager, HR, and Admin roles with appropriate permissions and views.

• Singapore-Compliant — Built with Singapore CPF rates, leave proration rules, and SGD currency formatting.

• Secure — Supports Google Sign-In and credentials-based authentication with JWT sessions.

Built for Tertiary Infotech Academy Pte Ltd.
